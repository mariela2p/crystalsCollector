Crystals Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific ammount of points to your total score.

You win the game by marching your total score to randome number, you lose the game if your total score goes above a randome number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
